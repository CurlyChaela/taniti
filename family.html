Taniti Heritage Museum

The Taniti Heritage Museum is a captivating cultural institution dedicated to preserving and showcasing the rich history of Taniti. Located in the heart of the island, the museum offers visitors an in-depth look at the island’s past, from ancient civilizations to modern developments. Through interactive exhibits, historical artifacts, and engaging displays, the Taniti Heritage Museum brings the island’s unique heritage to life, making it a must-visit destination for history enthusiasts and curious travelers alike.

Discover the Wonders of Taniti Island

Welcome to Taniti Island, a tropical paradise offering a diverse array of excursions for every visitor. Dive into adventure with our thrilling chartered fishing tours and vibrant snorkeling experiences. For an adrenaline rush, soar through the lush canopy on a rainforest zip-lining adventure or enjoy breathtaking views on a scenic helicopter ride. Indulge in indoor fun at Taniti's arcade in Merriton Landing, featuring a variety of classic and modern games. Immerse yourself in the local culture by visiting our art galleries, showcasing works by both indigenous and international artists. For a bit of friendly competition, head to the lively bowling alley, also located in Merriton Landing. Excitingly, Taniti will soon boast a nine-hole golf course near Yellow Leaf Bay, offering stunning views and a challenging play experience. Most of these activities are centered in Merriton Landing, a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay, making it the ultimate destination for adventure and entertainment.
Discover the endless possibilities on Taniti Island, where thrilling adventures and cultural experiences await.
