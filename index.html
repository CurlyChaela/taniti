About The Island

Taniti is a mesmerizing tropical island in the Pacific, spanning less than 500 square miles. Its diverse landscape features pristine sandy and rocky beaches, a secure harbor, lush tropical rainforests, and a mountainous region with a small, active volcano. Home to an indigenous population of approximately 20,000, Taniti's economy was historically based on fishing and agriculture. However, a recent surge in tourism is bringing new opportunities and growth to this enchanting island, making it a perfect destination for those seeking natural beauty and cultural richness.
