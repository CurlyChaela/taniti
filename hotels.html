Ocean Breeze Hotel

Experience unparalleled luxury at The Ocean Breeze Hotel, where the soothing sounds of the waves and the gentle sea breeze create an oasis of tranquility. Nestled along the pristine shores of Taniti, our resort offers stunning ocean views from every room, world-class dining options featuring fresh, local cuisine, and an array of activities to suit every traveler. Relax by our infinity pool, indulge in a spa treatment, or explore the vibrant marine life with our guided snorkeling tours. At The Ocean Breeze Hotel, every moment is a perfect escape.

Tranquil Tides Hotel

Immerse yourself in the serene beauty of Taniti at Tranquil Tides Hotel. Our boutique hotel combines modern elegance with island charm, offering guests a peaceful retreat surrounded by lush tropical gardens. Each room is thoughtfully designed to provide maximum comfort and privacy, with private balconies overlooking the stunning landscapes. Enjoy our rooftop bar with panoramic views, rejuvenate at our wellness center, or take a leisurely stroll along our private beach. Paradise Peak Hotel

Perched atop Taniti's highest vantage point, Paradise Peak Hotel offers breathtaking views of the island's lush valleys and azure waters. This exclusive retreat features spacious suites with luxurious amenities, private plunge pools, and personalized butler service. Guests can savor gourmet meals prepared by our renowned chef, explore the island's hidden gems with our guided excursions, or simply unwind in the serene ambiance of our infinity-edge pool. At Paradise Peak Hotel, we redefine luxury and provide an unforgettable experience tailored to your desires.
