TANITI

Transportation

Transportation to Taniti primarily relies on air travel, with the island served by a small airport capable of handling small jets and propeller planes. This convenient access attracts the majority of visitors who seek the island's serene beauty and vibrant culture. However, Taniti also welcomes guests arriving on a small cruise ship, which docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for a memorable overnight experience every week. Excitingly, Taniti is in the midst of expanding its airport infrastructure to accommodate larger jets, a development set to enhance accessibility and connect more travelers to this idyllic paradise in the coming years.

Ground Transportation

Ground transportation options on Taniti offer convenience and flexibility for visitors exploring the island. Taniti City benefits from a reliable public bus service operating from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily, providing easy access to key destinations within the city. For travel beyond the city limits, private buses offer efficient connections throughout the island. Taxis are readily available in Taniti City for those preferring a direct and convenient mode of transportation, while rental cars can be arranged through local agencies near the airport, catering to those seeking independent exploration. Additionally, cyclists can enjoy exploring the island's scenic routes with bike rentals available from multiple vendors, with helmets required by law for safety. Taniti City's flat terrain and pedestrian-friendly layout make it a delight to explore on foot, particularly around the charming area of Merriton Landing, where many tourists find accommodation and a plethora of attractions within easy walking distance.
