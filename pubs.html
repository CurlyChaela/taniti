TANITI

The Coconut Lounge

Nestled in the heart of Taniti, The Coconut Lounge offers a relaxed and vibrant atmosphere. Known for its tropical cocktails and live music, this pub is the perfect spot to unwind after a day of exploring. Enjoy a selection of local beers, delicious bar snacks, and a friendly crowd

Sailor's Rest

Overlooking the scenic Yellow Leaf Bay, Sailor's Rest is a cozy pub with a nautical theme. With its warm wooden interiors and panoramic views, it's a favorite among locals and tourists alike. Savor hearty pub fare, craft beers, and refreshing cocktails as you soak in the stunning sunset over the water.

Microbrewery: Taniti Island Brews

Situated in the bustling area of Merriton Landing, Taniti Island Brews is the island's premier microbrewery. Dedicated to crafting unique and flavorful beers, this microbrewery uses local ingredients to create brews that capture the essence of Taniti. Take a tour of the brewery, enjoy tastings of their latest creations, and relax in the spacious taproom with views of the brewing process. Taniti Island Brews is a must-visit for beer enthusiasts and those looking to experience the island's innovative craft beer scene.
